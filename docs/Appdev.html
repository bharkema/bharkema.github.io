To be able to create and configurate an app, make sure you are logged in to the clappform portal

When you are logged in to the portal, you are logged in as a user. To use the Dev tools, please click on the icon in the bottom left.
that says, show as dev
Whenever you are active a dev tools user, the bottom left box wil darken to show you that you are currently logged in as a dev. To be able to create an app, you'll first need to acces the app screen. This can be done by either looking for an exiting app in the searchbar in the middle of the screen . Or you can press the "show all" button below the searchbar to get acces to all apps that are currently aviliable on your portal.

To create a new app:
Scroll down to the last app of the page to see a + mark, if you dont have any available apps. You'll see the + mark at the top of your screen.

To create an app you'll need to fill in the "Add App" form, as is shown down below.
You can press the Add button whenever all the forms are filled

To create a widget, you'll first need to add a Goupe at the side bar, and then make a page, by clicking on the "Add Page button" When all the forms are filled you see a + mark in the middle of your screen.
